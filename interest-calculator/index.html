About the App

Interest Calculator Pro is a comprehensive financial calculator for calculating simple interest, compound interest, EMI, and managing loan records. Perfect for individuals, small businesses, lenders, and borrowers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate compound interest?
Go to the Calculator tab, enter your principal amount, interest rate, and select dates. Choose "Compound" from the interest type selector and select your compounding period (monthly, yearly, etc.). Tap Calculate to see results.
Can I track multiple loans?
Yes! Use the Book tab to maintain records of multiple loans. You can track both lending (money you've lent) and borrowing (money you've borrowed), add partial payments, and view complete transaction history.
How do I export my calculations?
After calculating, tap the Share button in the results card. You can export as PDF, share as text, send via WhatsApp, or save as an image.
What currencies are supported?
The app supports multiple currencies including Rupee (₹), Dollar ($), Euro (€), Pound (£), Yen (¥), and you can also set a custom currency symbol. Go to Settings to change your currency preference.
How do I calculate EMI?
Go to the EMI tab, enter your loan amount, interest rate (annual %), and tenure (years or months). Select your EMI start month and tap Calculate to see the complete amortization schedule with monthly breakdowns.
Is my data safe?
Yes, all your calculation data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal financial data to any external servers.
What's included in Premium?
Premium unlocks unlimited saved calculations, removes ads, provides advanced PDF export features, and gives you priority support. It's a one-time purchase with lifetime access.
