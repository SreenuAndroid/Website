Introduction

Interest Calculator Pro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Interest Calculator Pro is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Local Data Storage: All your calculation data, loan records, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers.
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics or tracking services.

Data Storage

All data created within the app (calculations, loan records, settings) remains on your device. When you uninstall the app, all data is removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for:

  • In-App Purchases: App Store/Google Play for premium features
  • Sharing: System sharing features (WhatsApp, email, etc.) when you choose to export or share data

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not control or take responsibility for third-party practices.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
