Frequently Asked Questions

Are your apps free to use?

Yes, all our apps are free to download and use. Some apps may offer optional in-app purchases for additional features, but core functionality is always free.

Do your apps collect personal data?

We prioritize your privacy. Our apps are designed to work offline whenever possible and do not collect personal information. Any data you create stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Which devices are supported?

Our iOS apps support iPhone and iPad running iOS 15.0 or later. Mac apps require macOS 12.0 or later. Android apps support Android 7.0 (Nougat) and above.

How do I request a refund?

For App Store purchases, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com. For Google Play purchases, visit the Google Play Store app and go to your purchase history. If you need help, contact us via email.

An app isn't working properly. What should I do?

First, try restarting the app and your device. Make sure you have the latest version installed. If the problem persists, please email us with details about the issue, your device model, and OS version.

Can I suggest a new app or feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Send your ideas to fairappsmobile@gmail.com and we'll consider them for future updates.
